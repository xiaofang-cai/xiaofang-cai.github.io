I am a Research Assistant Professor in the National Center of Applied Mathmatics (Shenzhen) at [Southern University of Science and Technology (SUSTech)](https://www.sustech.edu.cn), working within Prof. Huang Wei's research group.

My research sits at the intersection of human–AI collaboration, group decision-making, and the institutional dynamics of new technologies — asking how new entities (AI systems, multi-agent architectures, emerging methodologies) gain legitimacy within existing collaborative structures.

My current project, builds an experimental multi-agent system platform to study how different MAS architectures shape group decision processes across task types, drawing on Dual Influence Theory and the GDSS/GSS research lineage. 

In parallel, I am developing **AI-augmented qualitative research methods** as a methodological contribution to IS, and pursuing applied work on AI governance through advisory reports to the Shenzhen Decision Advisory Committee and industry collaborations with Huawei and the Shenzhen Credit Center.

A longer-term thread of my work uses business history methods (Chandlerian institutional frameworks, oral history) to study how Chaoshan/Teochew merchant networks evolved over the past century

I welcome conversations on any of the above. Feel free to reach me by [email](mailto:caixf@mail.sustech.edu.cn).
